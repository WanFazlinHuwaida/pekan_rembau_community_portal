About Us

About the Local Community / People

The heart of Rembau lies in its people. A warm, welcoming community built on unity, respect and shared values. Living side by side in harmony while preserving traditions and embracing progress [1].

Pekan Rembau is the administrative and commercial centre of the Rembau District in Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia [1]. Rich in history and cultural heritage, the town is well known for its close-knit community, traditional values, and the practice of Adat Perpatih [1]. Pekan Rembau offers visitors a unique blend of historical landmarks, local businesses, cultural attractions, and authentic Negeri Sembilan cuisine, making it a welcoming destination for both locals and tourists [1].

Our Community

The people of Pekan Rembau come from diverse backgrounds and live together in harmony while preserving local traditions and cultural identity [1]. Community spirit remains strong through activities such as gotong-royong, cultural celebrations, religious festivals, and local markets that bring residents together [1].

Location

Pekan Rembau is located approximately 25 km south of Seremban and is easily accessible via the North–South Expressway (EXIT 223 Pedas/Linggi) and the Seremban–Tampin Federal Road [1]. It serves as the main gateway to nearby villages, heritage sites, and eco-tourism attractions within the Rembau District [1].
