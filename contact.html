Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rembau famous for?

Rembau is renowned for its preservation of the Adat Perpatih (matrilineal customary law), historic timber architecture, beautiful natural landscapes like Jeram Toi Waterfall, and its delicious, spicy Masak Lemak Cili Api.

What is Adat Perpatih?

It is a unique customary system originating from the Minangkabau people, where lineage and inheritance are traced through the maternal line. It promotes consensus and community harmony.

When is the best time to visit Rembau?

Rembau can be visited year-round. However, visiting during cultural festivals (often mid-year) or fruit seasons (Durian season around June-August) offers a more vibrant local experience.

Where can I find local authentic food?

Local eateries around Pekan Rembau and Pedas are perfect for finding authentic dishes like Lemang, Rendang, and Rembau Beef Noodle. Visit our Food page for more details!

How can I join community activities?

You can refer to the Events calendar on this portal or directly contact the Majlis Daerah Rembau (MDR) office to inquire about upcoming public gotong-royong or cultural nights.
