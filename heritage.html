Customary Heritage

Adat Perpatih Matrilineal System

A unique customary law where lineage, clan inheritance, and land titles are passed down through the maternal bloodline, cultivating deep community respect.

The 12 Matrilineal Clans

Structure of governance divided systematically into 12 clans (*Suku*) led by their respective clan chiefs under the *Undang*.

Museum Directory

Muzium Rembau (Istana Raja Melewar)

A brilliant architectural masterpiece built entirely of timber without using a single iron nail. Located in a serene landscape, the museum offers an educational refuge into the governance of Luak Rembau.

Operating Hours: 8:30 AM – 5:00 PM (Monday – Sunday)
Location: Jalan Dato' Undang, 71300 Rembau, Negeri Sembilan
Contact: 06-685 3144 / muzium@rembau.gov.my
