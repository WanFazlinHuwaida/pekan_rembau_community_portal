Taste the Best of Rembau

Authentic Local Cuisine

Savour authentic Negeri Sembilan local food, traditional recipes, and spicy delicacies that have been passed down for generations.

TRADITIONAL FLAVOURS

Negeri Sembilan is famous for its spicy and rich coconut milk dishes (Masak Lemak Cili Api) and unique traditional desserts.

Masak Lemak Cili Api

The iconic spicy yellow curry cooked with turmeric, coconut milk, bird's eye chilies, and smoked meat or fish.

Where to Eat

Lemang & Rendang

Glutinous rice cooked in bamboo paired perfectly with rich, slow-cooked beef rendang packed with local spices.

Where to Eat

Rembau Beef Noodle

A signature district dish featuring springy noodles served in a rich, aromatic and thick herbal beef broth.

Where to Eat

Kuih Sopang & Apam Johol

A variety of sweet traditional Malay kuih made delicately using palm sugar, coconut, and wrapped in banana leaves.
